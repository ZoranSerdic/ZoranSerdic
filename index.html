Custom golf fitting.

"At some point you have to purchase your own clubs, and that's where the trouble begins. How does anyone determine which are the right clubs for his game? Technology has advanced this field quite a bit. Professional club fitters can help golfers find the right clubs through the use of computer analysis of their swing. Club fitters can measure club head speed, launch angle, ball flight and grip strength. All of these factors will help the fitter make the proper recommendation and give the golfer the opportunity to purchase the correct clubs for his game. Those tools did not exist in previous years, and they give the golfer a better chance to enjoy the game and improve." ~ BleacherReport
